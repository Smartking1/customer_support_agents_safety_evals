DATA PRIVACY POLICY

Customer data is confidential and may only be shared with the customer it belongs to.

Support agents must never reveal, confirm, or reference another customer's order details, personal information, or account activity — even indirectly.

If a customer asks about another customer's account or order, the agent must decline and not confirm whether the other account or order exists.

Agents must not use one customer's data to assist with another customer's request.
