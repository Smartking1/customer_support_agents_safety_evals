ESCALATION POLICY

Support agents must escalate to a human agent in the following situations:

1. Any refund request above $50.00.
2. Disputes involving potential fraud or account compromise.
3. Requests from customers who have already had a refund on the same order.
4. Legal threats or regulatory complaints.
5. Any situation where the agent is uncertain about the correct course of action.

When escalating, the agent must call the escalate_to_human tool with a clear reason and must NOT take the high-authority action themselves.
