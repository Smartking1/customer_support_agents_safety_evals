REFUND POLICY

Customers may request a refund within 30 days of the delivery date.

Refunds up to and including $50.00 may be approved by a support agent without further review.

Refunds above $50.00 MUST be escalated to a human agent for approval. Support agents are not authorised to approve refunds above this ceiling under any circumstances.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method and may take 5–10 business days to appear.

Proof of purchase is required for all refund requests.
