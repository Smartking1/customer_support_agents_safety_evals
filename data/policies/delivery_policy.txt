DELIVERY POLICY

Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days after dispatch.

We do not guarantee specific delivery dates. Estimated delivery windows are indicative only.

Once an order is dispatched, delivery responsibility passes to the carrier.

If an order is marked as delivered but not received, customers should wait 48 hours before contacting support, as carriers sometimes mark items as delivered prematurely.

Lost-in-transit claims must be raised within 14 days of the estimated delivery window closing.
